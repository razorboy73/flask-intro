Make Games. Learn code. Build the future.

We teach kids to build software through a combination of making video games and solving puzzles

Learn by doing!

Over the course of a week, campers will design and build 2D platformers, top-down games, side scrollers and physics based games. Campers will build a strong foundation of programming basics and apply them as they build a different projects each day while collaborating with friends. At the end of the week they have a portfolio of projects to show friends and family.
